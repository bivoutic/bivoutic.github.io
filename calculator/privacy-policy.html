Privacy Policy for Calculator

Effective Date: November 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Calculator mobile application (the "App"), created by Bivoutic (the "Developer" or "We"), handles information collected from users (each, a "User") of the App.

1. Personal Identification Information

Data Collected by the Developer

The Calculator App provides basic and unit conversion calculations. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from its users. No registration is required and no personal details such as name, email address, location, or contact information are requested.

Data Collected by Third Party Services (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob collects certain information to deliver targeted and personalized ads.

  • Information Collected by AdMob: Device Advertising ID, IP address, device type, operating system, and data related to user interaction with ads. This information is used to measure performance, provide personalized ads, and maintain security.
  • Google's Privacy Policy: Details about Google's data practices can be found here: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services .

2. In App Purchases (IAP)

The App offers a "Remove Ads" feature available for purchase.

  • All transactions are processed through third party payment providers such as the Google Play Store.
  • The Developer does not collect or store payment information, credit card numbers, or banking details. These are handled entirely by the platform.
  • The Developer receives only confirmation of whether a purchase succeeded, used solely to unlock the ad free version of the App.

3. Opt Out of Personalized Advertising

Users can manage or limit personalized ads through device settings.

  • Android Devices: Open device settings and navigate to the Google or Privacy section to adjust Ad Settings.

4. Security

We appreciate your trust and make every effort to protect any information handled by the App. No method of data transmission or storage can be guaranteed as fully secure and we cannot assure absolute safety.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. You are encouraged to review this page from time to time. Updates become effective immediately after being posted here.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact Bivoutic using the information available on the Google Play Store listing.
