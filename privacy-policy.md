## Privacy Policy for Calculator

**Effective Date: November 9, 2025**

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which the **Calculator** mobile application (the "App"), developed by **Bivoutic** (the "Developer" or "We"), collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the App.

***

### 1. Personal Identification Information

#### Data Collected by the Developer
The **Calculator** App is a tool for basic and unit conversion calculations. It **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data** from its users. We do not require users to register or provide any personal information, such as name, email address, location, or contact details.

#### Data Collected by Third-Party Services (AdMob)
The App uses **Google AdMob** for serving advertisements. AdMob is a third-party service that collects certain information to provide targeted and personalized advertisements.

* **Information Collected by AdMob:** This may include, but is not limited to, the device's **Advertising ID**, IP address, device type, operating system, and data about the user's interaction with the ads. This data is collected to measure ad performance, provide personalized advertisements, and for general security purposes.
* **Google's Privacy Policy:** The data collected by AdMob is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can find out how Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services by visiting: [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

### 2. In-App Purchases (IAP)

The App offers a "Remove Ads" feature available for purchase.

* We use third-party payment processors (e.g., Google Play Store) to handle all purchase transactions.
* We **do not collect or store** your payment information, credit card details, or banking information. That information is processed directly by the respective platform.
* We only receive confirmation from the platform regarding the success or failure of a purchase, which is used to unlock the ad-free features in the App.
