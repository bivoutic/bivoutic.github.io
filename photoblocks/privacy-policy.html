Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how PhotoBlocks handles your data. By using the application, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Data Collection and Usage

PhotoBlocks is designed with a "Privacy First" approach. We minimize data collection to ensure your experience is secure and private:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • Game Progress: Level progress, high scores, and daily auto-solve limits are stored locally on your device. This data is not synchronized to our servers unless you explicitly use a cloud-save feature provided by the operating system (e.g., Google Play Games).
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous technical data (such as crash reports) to improve the stability of the application. This data does not identify you personally.

2. Third-Party Services

The application interacts with third-party services to provide core functionality:

  • Unsplash API / Remote Images: When using Endless Mode, the app fetches images from third-party repositories. These services may log your IP address as part of standard web traffic protocols.
  • Image Saving: When you choose to save a completed puzzle image to your gallery, the app requires access to your device's media storage. This access is used solely for saving the specific image you requested.

3. Permissions

The application may request the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to download remote puzzle images.
  • Storage/Media Access: Required only for exporting and saving puzzle images to your device gallery.
  • Vibration: Used to provide haptic feedback during gameplay.

4. Children's Privacy

PhotoBlocks does not knowingly collect any information from children. Our puzzles and themes are intended for a general audience.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

6. Contact Us
